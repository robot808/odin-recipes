Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

The classic peanut butter & jelly sandwich is a timeless comfort food. It combines the delectable sweetness of fruit jelly with the savory richness of peanut butter, and serves it up as a sandwich for simplicity and versatility. It even has its own theme song!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon fruit jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter onto one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly onto the other slice of bread.
  3. Bring both slices together to form a sandwich.
