Garden Salad

Description

The classic garden salad is a light and healthy dish with nearly endless variation that can be easily prepared with ingredients you probably already have. Feel free to add to add or omit ingredients or toss with your favorite dressing in place of olive oil and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (or your favorite lettuce, chopped)
  • 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine greens, tomatoes, cucumber, carrot, and onion.
  2. When ready to serve, drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, add black pepper to taste, and toss to coat.
