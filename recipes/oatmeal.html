Oatmeal

Description

This hearty yet simple oatmeal recipe can help anyone get the perfect start to their day. It can be prepared in less than 15 minutes and can be customized to almost any taste with choice of milk base and fruit and/or nut toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp almonds, sliced
  • 1 large ripe banana, sliced

Steps

  1. Whisk together milk and sugar in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to low.
  2. Add rolled oats and simmer uncovered for 8-10 minutes, stirring ocassionally, until thick.
  3. Top with sliced almonds and banana (or toppings of choice) and serve warm.
